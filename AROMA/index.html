About Us

History of AMORA|AROMA

Born from a deep fascination with the cosmos and a love for storytelling through scent, AMORA|AROMA was founded by the Amora family, whose name, when reflected, becomes Aroma—a perfect metaphor for the duality of beauty: what we see and what we feel. The brand was conceived under a starlit sky, where inspiration flowed from the moon's shifting phases and the mystery of the stars. Each fragrance is an olfactory poem, capturing a moment in the heavens—a reminder that just like celestial bodies, beauty comes in phases and light.

Mission Statement

At AMORA|AROMA, our mission is to craft fragrances that transcend time and space—bottling the energy of the moon and the brilliance of the stars into every scent. We aim to ignite emotion, spark imagination, and awaken inner beauty through our cosmic-inspired perfumes.

Vision Statement

We envision AMORA|AROMA as a guiding star in the fragrance industry—a brand where art, astronomy, and aroma converge. Our dream is to become a global symbol of elegance and wonder, inspiring people to connect with the universe within and around them, one celestial scent at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, AMORA|AROMA began as a pursuit of celestial harmony in scent. The interplay between our name—Amora becoming Aroma when mirrored—represents our philosophy that true beauty flows in both directions: what we perceive and what we experience. Our artisanal approach combines traditional perfumery with innovative techniques inspired by the stars and celestial bodies that guide us.

Our Commitment

We believe in creating fragrances that tell a story—each bottle containing notes carefully selected to transport you to different realms of the cosmos. Using only the finest natural ingredients sourced from around the world, we ensure that every AMORA|AROMA creation is as unique as the individual wearing it.

Our Future

As we continue to grow, AMORA|AROMA remains dedicated to pushing the boundaries of olfactory art. We're constantly exploring new scent combinations and environmental practices that honor both our planet and the vast universe that inspires us. Join us on this journey through the stars, one fragrance at a time.
